Info

What are points

Points can be gathered by attending events like chalk army or flyer navy

Why do I want points?

Points can be redeemed for dues and other things

How many points do i need?

See the chart to find out what you can redeem your points for.

Point System

Meetings

1/2/4 pts

51 points/semester

1 point for the 1st meeting, 2 points for the 2nd meeting, and then 4 points for every consecutive meeting thereafter. Points will start over if a meeting has been missed

Events

25pts

100 points/semester

This can include: Halloween Party set up/tear down, game nights, Toriko nights, or any other event that Anime Party hosts

Chalk Army/ Flier Navy

35pts

105 points/semester

This is a group of people armed with fliers and chalk that roam campus and chalk the sidewalks and post fliers in the buildings advertising an Anime Party event.

Bringing Anime

2/4pts

78 points/semester

You can bring in an anime or more for 2 points. If your anime wins the vote to be watched you earn an additional 4 points. You cannot get more points by bringing in more than 1 anime.

Reviewing Anime

12pts

168 points/semester

This includes watching an anime and writing a short paragraph on why you liked or disliked the episode/anime, how it is similar/different in comparisons, or gives a detailed (not too detailed) summary. This review has a chance to be put in the monthly newsletter which will be rewarded 5 points.

Prizes

40/20/10/10

50 points maximum

This is for any sort of event that is being hosted for members only. It is a way to reward participants 10 points, and the event winners to get 10 points by being in 3rd place in the event, 20 points for 2nd, and 40 points for 1st, giving a grand total of 50 points overall for the event.

Spending Points

Raffle

Members can spend points to buy tickets during the monthly raffle. There will be 3 prizes given per month.

Japanese Candy/Junk Food

Members can also spend points on possible Japanese treats.
